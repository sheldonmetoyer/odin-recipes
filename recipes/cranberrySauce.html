Cranberry Sauce

Description

Oh the sweet taste of cranberry sauce. So simple, so sweet.

This easy recipe will leave your guest with their mouths watering for more.



Ingredients

  • 12 ounces cranberries
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup orange juice

Steps

  1. Gather all Ingredients
  2. Disolve sugar in the orange juice in a medium saucepan over medium heat
  3. Stir in the cranberries and cook until they start to pop, approximately ten minutes
  4. Remove from heat and place sauce in a bowl
  5. Enjoy!
