Sweet Potato Casserole

Description

Of all the ways to eat potatoes, sweet or not, this is my favorite

Topped with marshmellows, nice and mushy, you will be stuffed :D


Ingredients

Sweet Potatoes:

  • 4 cups peeled, cubed sweet potatoes
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 4 tablespoons butter, softened
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt

Pecan topping:

  • 1/2 cup packed brown sugar
  • 1/3 cup all-purpose flour
  • 3 tablespoons butter, softened
  • 1/2 cup chopped pecans

Steps

  1. preheat oven to 325 degrees F *165 degrees C
  2. Put sweet potatoes in a medium saucepan and cover with water. Cook for 10-15 minutes, drain and transfer to bowl
  3. Mash potatoes. Add eggs and mix. Add sugar, milk, vanilla and salt. Transfer to baking dish
  4. Make topping: mix brown sugar and flour. Cut in butter but don't over mix. stir in pecans. sprinkle on sweet potatoes
  5. Bake in preheated oven until lightly brown. approximately 30 minutes
  6. Enjoy! :D
